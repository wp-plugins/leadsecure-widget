Bring live two-way video, audio and chat communication to your website! LeadSecure is a web and mobile solution for real-time video communication. 

== Description ==

The plug-in is developed by LeadSecure Inc, whose mission is to bring face-to-face communication online. Host your own live video/chat sessions, interact with your visitors and boost your sales. 

LeadSecure plugin lets you embed ‘Video Chat' button, online and offline forms on your website to let your website visitors click to start a video or regular chat with you directly. Visitors can also make an audio or video call directly from their web browser.

You can use LeadSecure to:

- Add a video chat window in position of your choice on your site
- Instantly expand your business to out-of-area, out-of-state or even to global audience
- Get IM alerts for chats and video calls
- Answer questions on the spot, provide real-time help and support by chat or by initiating a live video session.
- No contact form to fill, no downloads of plug-ins, so your customers can approach you directly!
- Be proactive and engage users who are on your website
- Promote web services, products, real estate properties
- Increase your sales from better served customer base
- Reliable support video chat plugin and increased customer satisfaction
- Take audio and video calls from our mobile apps too
- States of presence - online/offline

== Installation ==

1. Upload the `leadsecure-plugin.php` file into the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory
2. Activate the plugin through the `Plugins` menu in WordPress
3. Edit the necessary fields in the `LeadSecure Settings` menu. If you don't have a UserID, please register for free at http://leadsecure.com/sign-up-for-free/. Leaving blank Front Message and Custom CSS file will use the default values.
4. Place the plugin where ever you need on your page using the tag [leadsecure_widget] or just call do_action('leadsecure_widget');
5. Enjoy!
